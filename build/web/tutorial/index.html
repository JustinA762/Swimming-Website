What are my Components and how does it work?

My Component is a container and item type of component with additional functionalities such as a Read More Read Less, a filter, Back to the top function and more. There are actually two Make js, MakeStroke (The container component) and MakeDrill (The item component).
MakeStroke takes two parameters: the title of the technique and the type of technique such as stroke or other
MakeDrill takes four parameters: the title of the Drill, the image of the Body Workout, the type of body workout, and the benefit which is the description of the drill.
